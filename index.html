Quirky IRC Client

Destined to be a real IRC client for both light and heavyweight IRC users. We do plan on supporting all IRCv3 extensions, and already have minimal internal implementations under way.

In short: Wanted a good looking IRC client that was still an IRC client, didn't have a shed-load of deps, and wasn't another IM library frontend. [1]

Currently under heavy development!

Important

The core library is missing a ton of checks, so wil likely crash in some situations. ircfuzz kills it instantly. Also note there is NO SSL validation and all certificates are accepted!
